World's Happiness Score 2023

The World Happiness Score assesses the world's most joyful nations by considering diverse factors like GDP per capita, life expectancy, acts of kindness, and views on corruption. This research involves surveying a representative group of individuals in each country, inviting them to rate their life satisfaction using a scale from 0 to 10, considering these various criteria. The collective happiness score for each nation is then determined by computing the average of these evaluations.

Key Happiness Determinants

Happiness scores are determined by key indicators like GDP per capita, Healthy Life expectancy, Generosity, and Perception of Corruption. Higher GDP per Capita and healthy life expectancy correlate with higher happiness scores. However, there's no significant link between generosity and happiness. Trust in government (Perception of Corruption) doesn't always align with happiness, except in Western European countries where high trust and happiness coincide.
